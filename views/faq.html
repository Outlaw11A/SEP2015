Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Does Labourhire handle payments?

A. No, Labourhire is a service to help employers and labourers find one another, payment must be arranged independently


Q. Is labourhire a government funded program?

A. No, Labourhire is run by an independent team of web developers

Q. What if there are issues with the work arrangement?

A. Labourhire takes no responsibility for the contact between workers and labourers. The rating system is designed to provide an indication of a users credibility.
