About Us
At Labourhire we know how difficult it can be to find good labourers at short notice.

We have made it our mission to help employers and labourers to find what they are looking for as quickly as possible.
How it works

Finding work or a labourer at short notice is easy with Labourhire.

Employers

Set up your profile and show off your qualifications. Browse advertisements from dozens of employers in your area and post offers to jobs you like the look of.
You will be rewarded for solid work with higher ratings, increasing your chances of being given jobs in the future, then you can just sit back and watch the jobs roll in.

Labourers

Set up a company profile and begin posting advertisements whenever those unexpected jobs creep up on you. You can expect to see offers within just minutes! Scan over the offers for someone with a high rating, or choose to give a newbie a chance to show their worth. As an employer, you are in control of who you hire.
